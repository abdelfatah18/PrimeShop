Can I return part of my order?

Yes, you can return individual items from your order. Each item will be reviewed separately, and eligible items will be refunded according to our return policy.

How long does the refund process take?

Once we receive and inspect your returned items, refunds are usually processed within 2–3 business days. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the amount to appear in your account.

Can I exchange an item for a different size or color?

Yes, exchanges are available for different sizes or colors, subject to stock availability. If the requested item is not available, a refund will be issued instead.
